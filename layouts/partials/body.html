This book dances through the patchwork of my journey, stitching together a colorful tapestry of experiences. From the heart-pounding energy of professional and college athletes to the sage wisdom of a speech and debate guru to the soul-stirring and vulnerable lyrics of Kris Kristofferson to the unexpected political rise of Ronald Reagan, my life has been a whirlwind of influences.

As an entrepreneur, I’ve taken many chances, each with its tale of conquest and calamity. I found my anchor through God’s unwavering love and patience in the stormy seas of prejudice. I offer glimmering pearls of wisdom within these pages, a North Star for those navigating uncertain waters. No matter where you are in your journey, think of this book as your trusty compass and steadfast companion on the grand adventure of life.
